BeautyBuy is a WooCommerce template pack for WordPress. It is designed for creating websites for beauty stores, cosmetic stores, beauty shops, beauty salons and skin care or hair care products. It can also be used to create spa or salon websites, or any WooCommerce websites for beauty, wellness, health related products and services.
